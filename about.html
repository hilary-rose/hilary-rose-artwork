About Hilary Rose

Hilary Rose is a Canadian artist originally from Toronto. She has also lived in Paris, France; London, England; Montréal, Québec; and currently resides in Ottawa.

Art has been part of her life since early on, and she has dabbled in a wide variety of media: charcoal, pencil, ink, watercolour, oils, acrylics, and most recently, gouache.

She enjoys painting a variety of subjects, including still life, city scenes, and figurative work.
